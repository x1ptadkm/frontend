Quan Ba - Ha Giang

About 46km north of Ha Giang city center. Coming to Quan Ba, Ha Giang, you will have the opportunity to enjoy the fresh air from an altitude of 1000m above sea level and immerse yourself in the romantic yet majestic nature. Quan Ba is likened to a miniature "Da Lat", a "common home" of 22 ethnic groups living together with their own customs, traditions, and beliefs that create uniqueness and attract tourists. guests that not everywhere can get.
