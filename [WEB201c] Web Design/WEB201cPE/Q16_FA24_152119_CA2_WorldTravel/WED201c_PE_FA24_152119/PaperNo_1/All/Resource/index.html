Famous Places in Korea

Nami Island

Nami Island was formed when the waters of the North Han River rose and flooded a land. Since then, a tourism company has developed the island and turned it into an amusement park. The island has a poetic and romantic landscape, attracting millions of tourists every year. Nami Di is famous not only for its natural scenery but also because it is the main filming location of the extremely famous drama "Winter Sonata".

Everland Park

Everland is a large amusement park in South Korea, established in 1976 and located in Yongin City, about 40km south of Seoul. With an area of ​​over 1,480,000 square meters, Everland is one of the largest amusement parks in Asia and offers a variety of entertainment for all ages. If you do not know what to do or where to stay in Korea, you should definitely not miss this wonderful entertainment paradise for a memorable vacation in the land of kimchi.
